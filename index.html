Create A Private Web App

Apps created here use the "Progressive Web App" technology built into most device browsers. Your app and its data are stored directly on your device, not available to the public, and not stored on this web server. If you want to share your app, provide your HTML code to the person you want to share it with and they can use this site to create their own version of your app to keep on their device.

Suggested Use:

  1. On mobile devices, first use the "Add to Home Screen" feature from your phone or tablet's browser to place this page on your device's home screen.
  2. Exit your browser and find the app icon you just put on your home screen. Open it. You'll see this page again.
  3. Use your favorite LLM like Claude or ChatGPT to generate an app, in your prompt, tell it to create the app "as a single HTML file" and "make it mobile friendly."
  4. Paste the code it generates below and give it an "App Name"
  5. Click the "View App" button (visible after code is pasted and the name is filled in) to start using your new custom app.
